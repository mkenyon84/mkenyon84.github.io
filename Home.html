Headteacher's Welcome


Welcome to our first ever live streamed Open Evening. We do appreciate that this will not fully do justice to what prospective parents and students would have experienced on our usual Open Evening but we hope that it will give you a glimpse of what the school has to offer your son, why it is such a special environment for boys to grow up in and why we are all so passionate about it.
My personal introduction has been pre-recorded but I will be available live at 7pm to answer any questions you may have.

Live Events 

A number of our subject areas are presenting live from their classrooms this evening. Please feel free to pop in and out of rooms as you would on a normal Open Evening. In each room, you will find teachers and some pupils conducting presentations, experiments, giving details about each subject and answering questions from the Zoom chat. Use the schedule or the subject pictures to navigate to different rooms at the given times. Most subjects will present more than once to hopefully give you a chance to see everything on your list.
